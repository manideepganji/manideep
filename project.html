HR Manager Operations - Hiring Process

1. Workflow Capabilities

Our system offers a streamlined and efficient workflow for managing the HR hiring process. Below are the steps involved:

  • Applicant submits resume with an opportunity ID.
  • Resume extraction and storage.
  • Opportunity ID verification and HR forwarding.
  • HR verifies and forwards to the manager.
  • Manager schedules interview.
  • Interviewer conducts interview and updates results.
  • Manager reviews and decides.
  • HR sends out offer letter if selected.
  • Applicant accepts or rejects.

2. Business Process Components

  • Applicant Interaction (Resume Submission)
  • HR Processing (Verification and Shortlisting)
  • Manager Evaluation (Interview and Feedback)
  • Decision Making (Offer or Rejection)

3. HR Hiring Process Data

Stage Description Assigned To Status
Resume Submission Applicant submits resume. Applicant Completed
Resume Extraction System extracts and stores resume. System Completed
HR Review HR verifies and shortlists candidates. HR In Progress
Manager Review Manager evaluates shortlisted candidates. Manager Pending
Interview Interview is conducted. Manager, Interviewer Pending
Offer Letter HR sends out offer letter. HR Pending
